Quick and effective management of an emergency requires a plan of action. This section provides you with the most widely accepted plan for handling first aid emergencies, and covers each essential step of the action plan in detail.

By the end of this section you should be able to:

Follow the steps in the Basic Life Support Flow Chart.
List the steps in the Emergency Action Plan.
Demonstrate the management of an unconscious casualty.
Recognise and clear an obstructed airway.
Demonstrate the management of a choking casualty.
Perform rescue breaths on all age groups.
Perform chest compressions on all age groups.
Perform CPR on all age groups.
List the steps in using an AED.
Demonstrate the safety use of an AED.
